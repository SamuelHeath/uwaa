The Competition

The Australian Universities Rocket Competition (AURC) is an inagural competition being organised by the Australian Youth Aerospace Association. It will take place in the eastern states early in 2019. The competition has two target heights of 10,000 ft. & 30,000 ft., the smaller of which is our target height. The competition is assessed based on a series of technical reports, as well as the launch, closeness to target height, and the successful recovery of the rocket.
